I want to upgrade the invoice workflow to support partial completion and partial delivery by quantity.

IMPORTANT:
This feature must be fully implemented for BOTH versions of the system:
- Light version
- Premium version

The same quantity workflow logic must apply to both versions.
Only the invoice field display may differ depending on version, but the workflow behavior must be identical.

Business case:
A single invoice may contain multiple dishdashas.
Example:
quantity = 7

Sometimes only 2 pieces are completed and delivered, while the remaining 5 are still under tailoring.

I want the system to support quantity-based workflow stages.

==================================================
1) CORE QUANTITY WORKFLOW
==================================================

Each invoice must track:

- total quantity
- quantity currently in progress
- quantity ready for pickup
- quantity already delivered
- quantity still remaining under tailoring

Suggested fields:

- totalQty
- inProgressQty
- readyQty
- deliveredQty
- remainingQty

remainingQty can be calculated or stored, as long as it is always accurate.

Formula:
remainingQty = totalQty - deliveredQty - readyQty (or based on stage quantities)

==================================================
2) PARTIAL COMPLETION BY STAGE
==================================================

At every workflow stage:
- cutting
- stitching / tailoring
- ironing / finishing
- ready for reception

the staff user must be able to complete only part of the quantity.

Example:
Invoice total = 7

Cutting finishes 2 pieces

After clicking complete:
- 2 pieces move to next stage
- 5 remain in current stage

The invoice must remain active.

==================================================
3) COMPLETE QUANTITY ACTION
==================================================

At each workflow stage, add a new action:

"Complete Quantity"

The staff should choose how many pieces are completed.

Example UI:

Available pieces at current stage = 7

Complete quantity:
[ 2 ]

After confirmation:
- move 2 to next stage
- leave 5 in current stage

Validation:
- cannot complete more than available
- cannot input zero or negative values
- cannot exceed invoice total

==================================================
4) RECEPTION / READY FOR PICKUP
==================================================

At reception, show:

- ready for pickup quantity
- still under tailoring quantity
- delivered quantity
- total quantity

Example:

Invoice #1051
Total: 7
Ready: 2
Under tailoring: 5
Delivered: 0

==================================================
5) PARTIAL DELIVERY
==================================================

Reception must be able to partially deliver.

Example:
2 pieces are ready
customer picks up 2

After delivery:
- delivered = 2
- under tailoring = 5
- invoice remains open

The invoice should NOT close yet.

==================================================
6) AUTO CLOSE INVOICE
==================================================

Invoice should only close automatically when:

deliveredQty == totalQty

Example:
7 delivered out of 7

Then invoice status becomes:
- completed
- delivered
- closed

==================================================
7) MOVEMENT HISTORY LOG
==================================================

Add quantity movement history.

Every movement must be logged with:

- date / time
- from stage
- to stage
- quantity
- user who performed action if available

Example:

03 Apr
cutting -> stitching (2)

04 Apr
stitching -> ready (2)

04 Apr
ready -> delivered (2)

This history should remain visible in the invoice details.

==================================================
8) UI DISPLAY
==================================================

Show quantity summary clearly inside invoice details.

Example:

Total quantity: 7
Delivered: 2
Ready: 0
Under tailoring: 5

A progress bar is preferred if possible.

Example:
2 / 7 delivered
